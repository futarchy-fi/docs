# For Investors and Grant Makers

Futarchy Labs is building market infrastructure for autonomous decision-making.

The work spans live conditional markets, agent-market research, Bayesian market
engines, and token-backed autonomous optimizer experiments. This makes it a fit
for several funding categories:

- prediction markets and information aggregation,
- AI agent evaluation and coordination,
- on-chain governance and treasury automation,
- mechanism design research,
- public-good infrastructure for credible decision systems.

## What is live

- `futarchy.fi` provides a public interface for conditional governance markets.
- Public repos document the protocol, interface, indexers, FAO contracts, agents
  research, and Bayes Market experiments.
- `bayes.futarchy.ai` exposes an experimental Bayesian prediction-market engine.

## What is being developed

- Permissionless market-creation flows.
- Better public runbooks for launching decision markets.
- Agent and PR prediction markets.
- Market-based reviewer and task-allocation mechanisms.
- Bayesian belief-network markets.
- FAO-style autonomous optimizer contracts and liquidity infrastructure.

## Why fund this

Better AI systems need better evaluation loops. Better organizations need better
ways to aggregate beliefs. Markets can turn distributed information into prices
that systems can act on.

Futarchy Labs is positioned at the intersection of:

- markets for beliefs,
- autonomous agent teams,
- crypto-native incentives,
- and programmable governance.
